Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers to some of the most common inquiries about Collaborative Canvas.

Collaborative Canvas is a powerful online platform designed to facilitate real-time collaboration and enhance learning. It offers a suite of integrated tools, including a dynamic whiteboard, simultaneous editing capabilities, comprehensive version history, and effective feedback mechanisms, enabling teams and learners to work together seamlessly and efficiently.

We offer a range of subscription plans to suit different needs, including a free basic plan with limited features. Our premium plans provide access to advanced functionalities and increased usage limits. Please visit our pricing page for detailed information on our plans and what they include.

Key features of Collaborative Canvas include:
  • Real-time collaborative editing
  • Interactive whiteboard with various tools
  • Comprehensive version history with rollback capabilities
  • Integrated feedback tools (annotations, comments, discussions)
  • Versatile file uploading (images, documents, presentations)
  • Secure user and permission management
